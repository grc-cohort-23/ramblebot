In the quiet morning light I wake to drifting thoughts across a softened sky
The world breathes gently as if it remembers something we have forgotten
I walk through moments that dissolve like mist upon warm stone paths
Each step becomes a memory not yet fully formed but already fading
There is a calm between heartbeats where silence learns to speak softly
I listen to the wind and it teaches me the shape of patience
Even shadows carry warmth when seen through eyes that refuse to rush
The sky opens slowly revealing colors that no name can hold
I learn that stillness is not empty but full of unseen motion
And every ending is only a quiet beginning wearing a softer face
I stand here without needing answers letting the world move through me
And in that gentle surrender I finally understand the language of becoming
I am here now
